Last Updated: May 11, 2026

At Ascenta3, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

  • Communications: Messages you send to us or through in-game features (if any).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we learn we have such data, we will delete it.

We do not collect sensitive personal data like precise location, school affiliation details, or health information unless voluntarily provided — and we discourage sharing unnecessary personal details.

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share data with schools or districts unless legally compelled.

04 Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for functionality. You can manage preferences through your browser settings. We do not use tracking for targeted advertising aimed at children.
